[CyberChallenge.IT](https://cyberchallenge.it/) is the first Italian cybersecurity training program for talented young people from high schools and universities. In this edition I acted as the main responsible for the project management and taught at [Politecnico di Bari](https://www.poliba.it/) to the cyberchallenge class as an expert teacher, teaching Software and Web security and training the class solving some CTFs, with the aim of building a strong team ready for the national competition.
